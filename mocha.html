Mocha is a chocolate-flavoured warm beverage is based on espresso and hot milk but with added chocolate flavouring.
Read on to learn how to make Mocha coffee easily at home without any special equipement.

How To Make Mocha At Home

Do not worry if you dont have a coffee machine, to make our Mocha coffee at home you won't need any fancy equipement.

Ingredients

  • Your favourite strong instant coffee
  • Milk or milk alternative
  • Sugar (to taste)
  • Water
  • Drinking chocolate or cocoa powder
  • Cream for topping (optional)
  • Chocolate sprinkles (optional)

Equipment

  • Kettle
  • Saucepan
  • Thermometer
  • A mug

Method

  1. Boil a cup of water in your kettle and add 2 teaspoons of your chosen instant coffee
    to your cup. If you have sugar in your coffee, add it to taste here.
  2. Then add 1 tablespoon of drinking chocolate or cocoa powder to your coffee.
  3. Add about 35ml of freshly boiled (not boiling) water to your instant coffee.
  4. Next, it’s time to steam the milk. Measure out 250ml of milk.
  5. Then grab a saucepan and your thermometer, add the measured amount of milk and
    then heat it slowly on a medium heat, stirring from time to time to ensure it doesn’t boil.
    If you’re using dairy milk, it will need to reach 65°C, 60°C for soy or 54°C for almond.
  6. Once the milk is steamed, pour it over the chocolate and coffee mix.
  7. Add whipped cream and chocolate sprinkles if you wish.
