Cappucino is an espresso based coffee drink originated in italy and traditionally prepared with steamed milk
Read on to learn how to make Cappucino coffee easily at home without any special equipement.

How To Make Cappucino At Home

Do not worry if you dont have a coffee machine, to make our cappucino coffee at home you won't need any fancy equipement

Ingredients

  • Your favourite instant coffee
  • Milk or milk alternative (to taste)
  • Sugar (to taste)
  • Water
  • A topping such as chocolate sprinkles, cinnamon,
    cocoa powder, or ground nutmeg

Equipment

  • Kettle
  • Saucepan
  • Thermometer
  • Cappuccino cup
  • Jar with lid or a milk frothing wand

Method

  1. Boil a cup of water in your kettle and add 2 teaspoons of
    your chosen instant coffee to your cappuccino cup. If you
    have sugar in your coffee, add it to taste here.
  2. Measure out the milk so it is about 1/3 of the cup.
  3. Now, you need to steam the milk. Grab a saucepan and
    your thermometer, add the desired amount of milk and
    then heat it slowly on a medium heat, stirring from time to
    time to ensure it doesn’t boil. If you’re using dairy milk, it
    will need to reach 65°C, 60°C for soy or 54°C for almond.
  4. Pour the steamed milk over your coffee.
  5. Now you’ve steamed your milk, you’ll need to froth milk
    too! Again, measure out 1/3 of your cup for milk. Choose
    from the two options listed below to froth your milk.
  6. Then add the foamed milk to your cappuccino cup and
    sprinkle over your topping.
  7. Sit back, relax and enjoy!
